Arweave Normalized Address Converter

Enter a wallet address (Solana, EVM, or Arweave) to get its normalized Arweave-compatible address.

Normalized address:

Normalized addresses are a 43-character base64url SHA-256 digest used across the Arweave ecosystem. Solana addresses map directly (the address is the public key). EVM addresses require recovering the public key from an on-chain outgoing transaction — addresses that have never sent a transaction cannot be normalized here.
